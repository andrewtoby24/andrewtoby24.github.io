Trivia

  • He was the first player to reach Valorant rank (now known as Radiant) in North America during the Closed Beta.
  • The Most kills made were 40 on Breeze during VCT 2022: North America Stage 2 Challengers in the Group Stage match against Luminosity Gaming.
  • He revealed that he is protanomaly colorblind, and as a result uses yellow instead of red as his enemy highlight color in Valorant.
  • First North America player to win a VCT Masters Event more than once, being VCT 2021 Stage 2 Masters Reykjavik and VCT 2024 Masters Madrid.
